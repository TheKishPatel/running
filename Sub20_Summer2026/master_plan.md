## Contingency Protocols

### Illness During Training

**Symptoms above neck (runny nose, mild sneezing):** Rest day 1. Easy test run day 2 if improving. Resume easy running day 3-4. Quality sessions day 5-6 if 100% clear.

**Symptoms below neck (chest, cough, phlegm, sore throat):** Complete rest until 100% symptom-free. Test run only after full clearance: 4km @ 6:00/km, HR <145 BPM, no symptoms during or 2 hours after. Easy running 3-5 days before resuming quality sessions.

**If illness in final 4 weeks (from 1 Jun):**
- 1-3 days lost: Continue plan, skip makeup sessions
- 4-7 days lost: Adjust race goal 15-30 seconds slower
- 8-14 days lost: Strongly consider postponing
- 14+ days lost: DNS/postpone

### Missed Sessions
- One session missed: Do NOT make up. Continue with next scheduled.
- Two consecutive sessions: Resume with easier version.
- Full week missed: Resume at -1 phase intensity, rebuild for 1 week.
- Two+ weeks missed: Restart from Phase 1, reassess race date.
